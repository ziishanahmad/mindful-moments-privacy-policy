Privacy Policy for Power Of Now-Quotes

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Power Of Now-Quotes respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

No Personal Data Collected

Power Of Now-Quotes does not collect, store, or share any personal information. You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

Offline Functionality

The app is fully offline. All quotes and fonts are bundled within the app package. No internet connection is required for usage, and no data is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

Permissions

Power Of Now-Quotes does not request or require access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or storage. The app only uses minimal system resources to display quotes and fonts.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party analytics, ads, or external SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Power Of Now-Quotes is safe for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, the app complies with COPPA and GDPR children’s privacy requirements.

Updates to This Policy

If the app changes to include online features or data collection in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
